About Project

Admin Role

In this project , the admin plays a crucial role in overseeing user management by creating, updating, and deleting user profiles and managing roles and permissions. They also handle data management tasks such as managing student and faculty data, course and curriculum management including course schedules and faculty assignments, attendance monitoring and reporting, grades and transcripts management, financial aspects like fee collection and scholarship management, communication within the system, system configuration and maintenance for smooth functioning, reporting and analytics for decision-making, and ensuring security measures and data privacy compliance.

Faculty Role

Faculty members in this project primarily focus on teaching and academic activities. Their roles include creating and delivering course materials, conducting lectures, grading assignments and exams, providing feedback to students, managing course schedules, organizing academic events, mentoring students, collaborating with other faculty members and administrators, participating in curriculum development and updates, conducting research, and contributing to the overall academic quality and growth of the institution.

Student Role

Students in this project are primarily focused on their academic journey. Their roles include accessing course materials, attending lectures and discussions, submitting assignments and projects, taking exams, receiving feedback from faculty, participating in extracurricular activities, managing their schedules and academic progress, interacting with faculty and peers, accessing academic support services, and actively engaging in learning to achieve their educational goals.
